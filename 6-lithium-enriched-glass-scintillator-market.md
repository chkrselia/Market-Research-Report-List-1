<p><p>The 6-Lithium Enriched Glass Scintillator market is segmented into three types based on lithium variants: Natural Lithium, Depleted Lithium, and Enriched Lithium. </p><p>Natural Lithium is extracted directly from natural sources and is used for its intrinsic scintillation properties. Depleted Lithium, which contains a lower concentration of the lithium-6 isotope, is utilized in applications where reduced interaction with neutron radiation is required. Enriched Lithium, having a higher concentration of lithium-6, offers enhanced sensitivity and efficiency for detecting thermal neutrons in scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
