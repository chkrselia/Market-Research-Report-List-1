<p><p>The aerators market is primarily divided into two categories: fixed aerators and floating aerators. Fixed aerators are stationary devices installed at a specific location to enhance oxygen transfer in water bodies, ideal for wastewater treatment and aquaculture ponds. In contrast, floating aerators are portable units that float on the water surface, promoting aeration by agitating the water. Both types serve the crucial purpose of improving water quality and supporting aquatic life by increasing dissolved oxygen levels.</p></p>
