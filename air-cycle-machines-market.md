<p><p>Air Cycle Machines (ACMs) are categorized based on their design and operational complexity. </p><p>- **Simple Cycle** utilizes a basic setup for air compression and expansion, offering straightforward operation. </p><p>- **Two-wheel Bootstrap** features additional components to enhance efficiency through cyclic operation. </p><p>- **Three-wheel** introduces a more complex system for improved performance and energy recovery. </p><p>- **Four-wheel/Dual-spool** systems optimize efficiency and performance further, using dual spools for better flow control and reduced losses, making them suitable for advanced applications.</p></p>
