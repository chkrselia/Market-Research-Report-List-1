<p><ul><li>Oil-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alloy cutting fluid market is primarily categorized into oil-based and water-based types. Oil-based cutting fluids, typically consisting of mineral oils and additives, offer excellent lubrication and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance machining. Conversely, water-based cutting fluids, often emulsions or solutions, provide effective cooling and are more environmentally friendly. They are suited for a variety of applications and minimize health risks associated with oil exposure, making them increasingly popular in modern manufacturing processes.</p></p>
