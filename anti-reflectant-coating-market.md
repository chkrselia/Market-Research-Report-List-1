<p><ul><li>Single-layer Interference Coating</li><li>Multi-layer Interference Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-reflectant coating market is primarily divided into two types: single-layer interference coatings and multi-layer interference coatings. Single-layer coatings employ a single thin film to reduce reflections by exploiting destructive interference, suitable for specific wavelengths. In contrast, multi-layer interference coatings consist of multiple layers of differing refractive indices, enabling broader wavelength coverage and enhanced performance across a range of applications. These coatings are crucial in optics, electronics, and solar energy sectors, improving light transmission and overall efficiency.</p></p>
