<p><p>The artificial bone market encompasses various types, including ceramics, composites, polymers, hydroxyapatite, and others. Ceramic materials offer biocompatibility and structural support, making them ideal for bone repair. Composites combine different materials to enhance mechanical properties and integration with natural bone. Polymers provide flexibility and customization for specific applications. Hydroxyapatite mimics natural bone mineral, promoting osteoconductivity and integration. Other materials may include metals and bioactive glasses, contributing to a diverse array of options for orthopedic and dental applications.</p></p>
