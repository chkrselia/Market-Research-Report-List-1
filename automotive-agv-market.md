<p><p>The Automotive Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) market is segmented into three main types: Unit Load Type, Automated Forklift Type, and Tugger Type. </p><p>Unit Load AGVs transport heavy goods or products, typically on pallets, facilitating streamlined material handling. Automated Forklift AGVs mimic traditional forklifts for loading and unloading tasks, enhancing efficiency in warehouse operations. Tugger Type AGVs pull trailers or carts, transporting materials along predefined paths, ideal for assembly lines and large facilities. Together, these types optimize logistics in the automotive industry.</p></p>
