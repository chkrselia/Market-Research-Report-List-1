<p><p>An automotive cornering lamp is a type of auxiliary lighting system designed to improve visibility during low-speed maneuvers, particularly while turning. These lamps enhance the illumination of corners, allowing drivers to see pedestrians, cyclists, or obstacles that may not be visible with standard headlights. Typically integrated into the vehicle's headlight assembly, cornering lamps activate when the vehicle makes a turn, providing additional light in the direction of the turn.</p><p>The automotive cornering lamp market is experiencing significant growth, driven by advances in automotive lighting technologies and increasing consumer preference for enhanced safety features.
