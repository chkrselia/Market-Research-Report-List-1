<p><p>The Biological Mass Spectrometry market features several ionization techniques, including Electrospray Ionization (ESI) and Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption Ionization (MALDI). ESI is commonly used for analyzing large biomolecules in solution, promoting the generation of charged particles for analysis. MALDI, on the other hand, is ideal for analyzing proteins and polymers in solid samples by using a laser to ionize matrix-assisted samples. Other techniques may include Atmospheric Pressure Chemical Ionization (APCI) and Fast Atom Bombardment (FAB), offering diverse applications in biological research.</p></p>
