<p><p>The Buffer and Line Driver market consists of three main types: Three States, Open Drain, and Standard. </p><p>Three States configurations allow outputs to be in high, low, or high-impedance states, enabling multiple devices to share a common line without interference. Open Drain outputs can only pull low, requiring external resistors to pull high, making them ideal for wired-AND logic applications. Both types enhance signal integrity and device interconnectivity in various electronic applications, optimizing performance in communication and data transfer systems.</p></p>
