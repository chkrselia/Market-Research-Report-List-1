<p><p>The Cephalosporin Antibiotics API market is segmented into four generations based on their spectrum of activity and bacterial resistance. First-generation cephalosporins are effective against gram-positive bacteria and some gram-negatives. Second-generation offers broader gram-negative coverage. Third-generation cephalosporins have enhanced activity against resistant strains, including certain gram-negative and some gram-positive bacteria. Fourth-generation cephalosporins provide extended spectrum, battling resistant organisms, and often featuring better penetration into the central nervous system, making them suitable for treating severe infections.</p></p>
