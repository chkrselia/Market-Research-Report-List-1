<p><p>The conveying equipment market can be categorized into three main types: bulk handling, unit handling, and parts and attachments. Bulk handling equipment is designed to transport large volumes of materials, such as grains or minerals, often in a continuous flow. Unit handling refers to equipment that moves individual items, like pallets or packages, facilitating easier sorting and storage. Parts and attachments encompass components that enhance or support these systems, such as belts, rollers, and conveyors, improving efficiency and adaptability in material handling processes.</p></p>
