<p><strong>The Conveyor Sorting Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conveyor sorting systems are essential in automating product distribution in logistics and manufacturing. Linear sortation systems operate on a straight path, efficiently directing items to designated areas using slats or belts. In contrast, loop sortation systems feature a continuous, circular track that allows for smoother item transport and higher throughput. Both systems enhance operational efficiency, reduce manual handling, and improve accuracy in sorting tasks, catering to various industries such as e-commerce, retail, and parcel delivery.</p></p>
