<p><p>Dielectric films for the capacitor market include various types based on materials and applications. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) capacitor films are known for their high insulation resistance and low dielectric loss, making them popular in AC applications. BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) films offer superior thermal stability and mechanical properties, ideal for high-frequency use. PI (Polyimide) capacitor films are recognized for their excellent thermal and chemical resistance, suitable for extreme conditions. Other films may include materials like polyester, serving diverse industry needs.</p></p>
