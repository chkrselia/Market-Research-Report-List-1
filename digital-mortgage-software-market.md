<p><p>Digital mortgage software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and accessibility from any device with internet access. It often features automatic updates and lower upfront costs. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers and provides greater control over data security and customization but typically requires higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type addresses different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
