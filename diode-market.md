<p><p>The diode market consists of several key types, each serving distinct functions. Schottky diodes are known for their fast switching and low forward voltage drop, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. TVS diodes protect circuits from voltage spikes. Switching diodes are commonly used in signal routing, while Zener diodes provide voltage regulation. Rectifier diodes convert AC to DC power. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses specialized diodes like laser diodes and photodiodes, catering to niche applications across various industries.</p></p>
