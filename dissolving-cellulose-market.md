<p><strong>The Dissolving Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Wood Pulp Or Sulfate Wood Pulp</li><li>Wood Pulp Of Sulfite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dissolving cellulose market includes various types of wood pulp used to produce cellulose derivatives. Alkali wood pulp, or sulfate wood pulp, is produced through the kraft process, using sodium hydroxide and sodium sulfide, yielding strong, versatile fibers. Sulfite wood pulp is made using sulfurous acid and bisulfites, resulting in softer, purer fibers ideal for textiles and films. Both types cater to industries like textiles, pharmaceuticals, and food, with sulfate pulp being more robust and sulfite pulp offering higher purity and solubility.</p></p>
