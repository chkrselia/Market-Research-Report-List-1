Key market types include Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS), which measures temperature variations along the cable length, and Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), used for detecting vibrations and sounds, making it valuable for security and monitoring applications. Other technologies, such as Distributed Strain Sensing (DSS), also contribute to the market by providing insight into stress and structural integrity in various industries.</p></p>
