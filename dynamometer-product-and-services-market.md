<p><strong>The Dynamometer Product and Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption</li><li>Transmission</li><li>Motoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamometer products and services are categorized into three primary market types: absorption, transmission, and motoring. The absorption market focuses on systems designed to measure and absorb torque, often used in engine testing. The transmission market involves dynamometers that evaluate the performance of gearboxes and drive systems, assessing power transfer efficiency. The motoring market includes dynamometers that provide power input to test and simulate operational conditions of components, such as electric motors, allowing for performance analysis and optimization.</p></p>
