<p><ul><li>Motor Start Capacitors</li><li>Motor Run Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric motor capacitors market is primarily segmented into two main types: motor start capacitors and motor run capacitors. Motor start capacitors provide a boost of energy to start electric motors, enabling them to overcome initial inertia. These are typically used in applications requiring high starting torque. In contrast, motor run capacitors are employed during continuous operation, enhancing efficiency and power factor, while stabilizing voltage fluctuations. Both types are essential for optimizing motor performance in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
