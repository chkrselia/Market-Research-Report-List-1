<p><strong>The Electric Thermal Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Closed Type</li><li>Normally Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Thermal Actuator market is primarily segmented into Normally Closed Type and Normally Open Type actuators. Normally Closed Type actuators maintain a closed position when de-energized, making them ideal for safety applications where systems should default to a safe state. Conversely, Normally Open Type actuators remain open when de-energized, suitable for scenarios that require a system to run without constant power. Both types cater to diverse applications in HVAC systems, industrial automation, and fluid control processes.</p></p>
