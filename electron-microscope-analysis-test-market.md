<p><p>The Electron Microscope Analysis Test market is primarily categorized into three types: Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM), Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM), and others. TEM provides detailed images by transmitting electrons through a thin specimen, revealing internal structures at atomic resolution. SEM, on the other hand, scans the surface of a sample with focused electrons, producing detailed three-dimensional images. The “Others” category includes various specialized electron microscopy techniques or hybrid systems, catering to diverse analytical needs across research, materials science, and biomedical applications.</p></p>
