<p><p>The fluorescent pigment powder market is categorized into thermoset, thermoplastic, and other types like aqueous dispersions. Thermoset pigments are heat-resistant and durable, ideal for coatings and plastics requiring high stability. Thermoplastic pigments are heat-moldable, suitable for applications like injection molding and flexible plastics. Other types, such as aqueous dispersions, are water-based and used in eco-friendly paints, inks, and textiles. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing performance, environmental considerations, and application versatility.</p></p>
