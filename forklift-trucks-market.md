<p><p>The forklift truck market is divided into several classes based on their design and application. Class 1 forklift trucks are electric motor riders designed for indoor use. Class 2 includes narrow-aisle trucks, suitable for confined spaces, often utilized in warehouses. Class 3 forklifts are electric motor hand trucks for lightweight loads. Classes 4 and 5 cover internal combustion trucks, ideal for outdoor use and heavy loads, with Class 4 using solid tires and Class 5 featuring pneumatic tires for better traction.</p></p>
