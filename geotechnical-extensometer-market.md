<p><strong>The Geotechnical Extensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ROD EXTENSOMETERS</li><li>PROBE EXTENSOMETERS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotechnical extensometer market is primarily segmented into rod extensometers and probe extensometers. Rod extensometers measure ground movement using a mechanical rod to track soil or rock displacement over time, making them suitable for assessing stability in engineering projects. Probe extensometers, on the other hand, utilize inclinometer or piezometer techniques to monitor underground displacement and pore pressure in real-time. Both types are critical for evaluating geotechnical conditions, enhancing safety, and ensuring structural integrity in various construction and mining applications.</p></p>
