Single Glazing consists of a single glass pane, offering basic insulation. Double Glazing features two panes with a sealed air gap, significantly enhancing thermal performance and noise reduction. Triple Glazing includes three panes, providing superior insulation and energy efficiency, ideal for extreme climates. Each type caters to different needs, with increasing layers translating to better energy savings and comfort.</p></p>
