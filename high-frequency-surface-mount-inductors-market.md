<p><p>The High Frequency Surface Mount Inductors market is comprised of three main types: wire wound, film, and multilayer inductors. Wire wound inductors utilize coiled wire for high inductance and efficiency, making them suitable for various RF applications. Film inductors employ thin layers of conductive materials, offering stability and precision, ideal for high-frequency circuits. Multilayer inductors are constructed with multiple layers of dielectric and conductive materials, providing compact size and high performance, making them popular in modern electronic devices.</p></p>
