<p><p>Horizontal baggers are categorized into two main market types based on their motion: Continuous Motion and Intermittent Motion. Continuous Motion horizontal baggers operate seamlessly, allowing for uninterrupted production flow and higher output rates, ideal for high-volume operations. In contrast, Intermittent Motion baggers operate in a stop-and-go manner, suitable for lower volume or diverse product runs, providing flexibility. Both types cater to different production needs, enhancing efficiency in packaging processes across various industries.</p></p>
