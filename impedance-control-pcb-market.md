<p><strong>The Impedance Control PCB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Ended Impedance</li><li>Differential Impedance</li><li>Coplanar Impedance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Impedance Control PCB market is segmented into three main types: Single-Ended Impedance, which involves one signal path with a reference ground; Differential Impedance, where signals are paired in a balanced manner, reducing noise and enhancing performance; and Coplanar Impedance, characterized by its signal and ground layers placed on the same plane, optimizing space. Each type serves distinct applications, with varying performance requirements in industries such as telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive, driving tailored designs for signal integrity.</p></p>
