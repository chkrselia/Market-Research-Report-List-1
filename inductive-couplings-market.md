<p><ul><li>Axail</li><li>Radial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive couplings are classified into axial and radial market types based on their alignment and application. Axial couplings involve magnetic fields aligned along a central axis, making them suitable for applications like motors and generators. In contrast, radial couplings feature magnetic fields that radiate outward from a central point, often used in wireless charging systems and RFID technology. Both types leverage electromagnetic induction to transfer energy wirelessly, catering to various industries and enhancing technological innovation.</p></p>
