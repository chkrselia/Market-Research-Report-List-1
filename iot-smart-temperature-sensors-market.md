<p><p>The IoT Smart Temperature Sensors market is divided into two main types: contact sensing and non-contact sensing. </p><p>Contact sensing devices directly measure temperature through physical contact with the object, offering high accuracy; they are often used in industrial applications. Non-contact sensing devices, on the other hand, detect temperature from a distance using infrared technology, making them ideal for sensitive environments or moving objects. This segmentation caters to various applications, enhancing efficiency in sectors like healthcare, manufacturing, and smart homes.</p></p>
