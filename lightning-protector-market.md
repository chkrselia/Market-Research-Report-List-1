<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Combination Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lightning Protector Market comprises three primary types: Voltage Switch Type, Pressure Limiting Type, and Combination Type. Voltage Switch Type devices operate by interrupting electrical currents when voltage exceeds a specific threshold, safeguarding systems from surge damage. Pressure Limiting Type protect against overpressure events, ensuring equipment integrity during lightning strikes. Combination Type integrates both voltage and pressure management, offering comprehensive protection. Each type caters to specific needs in various applications, ensuring enhanced safety and reliability in electrical systems.</p></p>
