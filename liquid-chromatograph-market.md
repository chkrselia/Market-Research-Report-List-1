<p><p>The liquid chromatography market consists of three main types: High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), Ultra High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC), and Medium-Performance Liquid Chromatography (MPLC). HPLC offers high resolution and efficiency for separating complex mixtures, making it widely used in pharmaceuticals and chemicals. UHPLC enhances speed and resolution further, ideal for advanced analytical applications. MPLC serves as a versatile option for less demanding separations, balancing cost and performance, primarily used in preparative tasks where higher throughput is needed.</p></p>
