<p><p>M2M services encompass various market types, primarily cellular and satellite. Cellular M2M services utilize mobile network infrastructure to enable real-time data transmission between devices, often providing widespread coverage and low latency, suited for applications like smart meters and vehicle tracking. In contrast, satellite M2M services leverage satellite networks to connect devices in remote or underserved areas where cellular coverage is limited. This is crucial for applications like environmental monitoring and fleet management in challenging terrains, ensuring global connectivity.</p></p>
