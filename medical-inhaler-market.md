<p><p>The medical inhaler market is categorized into several types, including Pressurized Metered Dose Inhalers (PMDIs), Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs), and others. PMDIs release a pre-measured dose of medication in aerosol form, suitable for immediate use. DPIs deliver medication in a dry powder form, requiring patients to inhale forcefully for effective administration. Other inhaler types may include nebulizers and soft mist inhalers, each offering varied features tailored to different patient needs and preferences in managing respiratory conditions.</p></p>
