<p><p>The Mesh Wi-Fi routers market is categorized into Bi-Band, Tri-Band, and Quad-Band systems. Bi-Band routers operate on two frequencies (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz), providing basic connectivity for most households. Tri-Band systems add an additional 5 GHz band, enhancing capacity and reducing congestion, ideal for larger homes or multiple devices. Quad-Band routers further extend this concept by introducing another 5 GHz band along with a 2.4 GHz band, delivering optimal performance for heavy usage environments with numerous connected devices.</p></p>
