<p><p>The modular camera system market is primarily divided into CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) modules and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) modules. CMOS modules are favored for their low power consumption, high-speed processing, and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for consumer electronics. In contrast, CCD modules provide superior image quality and sensitivity, often utilized in professional photography and scientific applications. Both types cater to diverse needs in various sectors, from surveillance to automotive, enhancing imaging capabilities across multiple platforms.</p></p>
