<p><p>Moisture barrier tapes are essential for preventing water vapor infiltration in construction and packaging. Polyethylene tapes are durable, flexible, and resistant to moisture, ideal for sealing joints. Asphalt-impregnated tapes combine asphalt with reinforcing materials for enhanced waterproofing. Asphalt-coated kraft paper tapes offer strength and moisture resistance, often used in roofing. Foil scrim kraft tapes feature a foil layer for added durability and vapor barrier properties. Paper-backed aluminum tapes provide excellent moisture resistance and thermal insulation, commonly used in HVAC systems. Each type caters to specific moisture protection needs.</p></p>
