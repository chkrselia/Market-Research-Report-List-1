<p><ul><li>Solid Guide Wire</li><li>Wrapped Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurovascular guidewires market can be categorized into two main types: solid guidewires and wrapped guidewires. Solid guidewires are designed to provide rigidity and stability, allowing precise navigation through the vascular system during interventional procedures. In contrast, wrapped guidewires feature a flexible, multi-layered design that enhances maneuverability and reduces vessel trauma. Both types serve essential roles in neurovascular interventions, facilitating access to complex anatomical structures and improving the overall efficacy and safety of medical procedures.</p></p>
