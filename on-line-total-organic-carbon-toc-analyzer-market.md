<p><p>The On-line Total Organic Carbon (TOC) Analyzer market features various methodologies for measuring organic carbon levels in water and wastewater. Ultraviolet Oxidation employs UV light to oxidize organic compounds, providing quick results. UV Persulfate Oxidation uses UV light in conjunction with persulfate to enhance the oxidation process, effectively analyzing even low concentrations of TOC. High-temperature Combustion involves combusting organic materials at elevated temperatures, converting them into CO2 for precise measurement, offering high accuracy for complex samples.</p></p>
