<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle tire market primarily features two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are constructed with fabric plies arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, providing better grip, improved fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. In contrast, bias tires have plies arranged diagonally, offering a more robust structure but typically resulting in lower performance and shorter lifespan. Radial tires dominate the market due to their superior handling, durability, and comfort, making them the preferred choice for modern vehicles.</p></p>
