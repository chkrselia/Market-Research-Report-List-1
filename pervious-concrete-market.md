<p><p>The pervious concrete market is categorized based on void content and its impact on performance characteristics. Low porosity pervious concrete, with 20% ± 5% voids, offers higher strength and durability, making it ideal for load-bearing applications. In contrast, high porosity pervious concrete, with 30% ± 5% voids, provides enhanced water permeability but at the cost of reduced strength. This type is suitable for applications where efficient water drainage is crucial, such as in parking lots and pathways.</p></p>
