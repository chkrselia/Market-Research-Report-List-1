<p><p>The prefabricated modular data center market is categorized into containerized and non-contained types. Containerized data centers are portable units, typically housed in standard shipping containers, providing flexibility and rapid deployment. They are ideal for temporary or remote installations. Non-contained data centers, on the other hand, are built within dedicated, modular frameworks that offer scalability and customization without the transportability of containers. Both types enhance efficiency and reduce construction time while catering to various operational needs.</p></p>
