<p><p>Thermal spray coatings are advanced surface engineering techniques used to apply protective or functional coatings to various materials. The process involves heating feedstock materials, such as metals, ceramics, or polymers, to a molten or semi-molten state and then spraying them onto a substrate at high velocity. This creates a durable, high-performance coating that enhances properties like wear resistance, corrosion protection, thermal insulation, and electrical conductivity. These coatings are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, energy, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p><p>The Thermal Spray Coatings Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for advanced materials in industrial applications and the need for cost-effective solutions to extend the lifespan of components.
