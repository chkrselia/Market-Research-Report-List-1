<p><p>The towed mower market features three main types: drum mowers, disc mowers, and sickle bar mowers. Drum mowers utilize rotating blades mounted on drums for efficient cutting, ideal for varied terrain. Disc mowers employ multiple circular blades that rotate to deliver a clean cut, making them suitable for larger fields and dense grass. Sickle bar mowers use a series of reciprocating blades to slice through vegetation, traditionally favored for their simplicity and effectiveness in maintaining hayfields and pastures.</p></p>
