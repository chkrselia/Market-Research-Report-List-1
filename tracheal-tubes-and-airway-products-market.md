<p><p>The tracheal tubes and airway products market encompasses various medical devices used to secure and maintain an open airway in patients. Endotracheal tubes facilitate ventilation directly into the trachea, while tracheostomy tubes provide long-term airway access. Breathing circuits connect these tubes to ventilators, ensuring effective gas exchange. Oral and nasopharyngeal airways help prevent airway obstruction, and laryngeal masks create a seal above the trachea for ventilation during anesthesia, enhancing patient safety and respiratory management across various healthcare settings.</p></p>
