<p><strong>The Vehicle Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle speed sensor market includes two primary types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors rely on magnetic fields to detect the rotational speed of components, providing precise speed measurements while being resistant to environmental factors. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors use electromagnetic induction to generate voltage proportional to the speed, making them simple and cost-effective. Both types are essential for vehicle performance and safety, enabling accurate speed monitoring and efficient operation of various automotive systems.</p></p>
