<p><ul><li>Biaxial Tension</li><li>Uniaxial Tension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The warp knitted geogrid market is primarily divided into two types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension geogrids. Biaxial tension geogrids are designed to provide strength and stability in two directions, making them ideal for applications such as base reinforcement and soil stabilization. In contrast, uniaxial tension geogrids focus on offering strength primarily in one direction, often used in retaining walls and slope stabilization. Each type caters to specific engineering needs, enhancing load distribution and soil integrity.</p></p>
