Cold Water Soluble PVA films dissolve in low-temperature water, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal thermal exposure. In contrast, Hot Water Soluble PVA films dissolve in high-temperature water, suitable for industrial processes and environments where heat is prevalent. Both types are eco-friendly alternatives for packaging, offering convenience and reducing plastic waste.</p></p>
