<p><p>The wireless telemetry system market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel systems transmit data from a single sensor to a receiver, making them suitable for simple applications where only one variable needs monitoring. In contrast, multi-channel systems can transmit data from multiple sensors simultaneously, allowing for real-time monitoring of various parameters. This versatility makes multi-channel systems ideal for complex applications in industries like healthcare, agriculture, and environmental monitoring where comprehensive data collection is essential.</p></p>
