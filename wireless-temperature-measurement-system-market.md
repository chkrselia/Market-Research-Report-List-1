<p><p>The Wireless Temperature Measurement System market comprises two primary types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact systems utilize infrared sensors to measure temperature from a distance, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. In contrast, contact type systems require direct attachment to the object to gauge temperature accurately, often using thermocouples or thermistors. Both types are widely employed in various industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and food processing, to enhance efficiency and safety in temperature monitoring.</p></p>
